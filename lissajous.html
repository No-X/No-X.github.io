Parameters that can be changed in url:
f - framerate
d - drag
a - starting angle
r - first distance (description in image)

?f=30&d=0.1&a=40&r=0.96 (default)
?f=100&d=0&a=40&r=0.96
?f=100&d=0.1&a=40&r=0.8
?f=100&d=0.1&a=40&r=0.6
?f=100&d=0.04&a=40&r=0.51
?f=100&d=0.1&a=40&r=0.5
?f=100&d=0.1&a=40&r=0.2
